Welcome to the Party Capital of India. Everything that is integrated to Goa, be it its cuisine or beaches, handicrafts or culture, fantastic and unique in itself.
Forget the beaches of Australia, Thailand or Bali and head to some of the most amazing beaches of the world in Goa. This former Portuguese colony boasts sun-kissed beaches that offer everything from parties to adventure, solitude to sunbathing.
Sun, surf, sand and awesome cuisine — Goa’s beaches offer them all.

The Goan coastline provides golden sun covered crescents of sand. The magnificent scenic beauty and the architectural splendours of its temples, churches and old houses have made Goa a firm favourite with travellers around the world. Goa has a soul which goes deep into unique history, rich culture and some of the prettiest natural scenery that India has to offer.

So be ready to witness all that Goa has to offer in the BITSMUN BEACH PARTY! We promise a night of great fun and excitement to all our participants. Details will be conveyed soon.
