Accommodation


Our Hospitality Partner

Ocean Residency, Goa


Accommodation details:

  • Accommodation will be provided to delegates attending the conference and will be from 2nd Feb to 5th Feb, 2017 (4 day 3 nights).
  • Price for accommodation is INR 2200 for 4 days and 3 nights in which entry to the social nights, travel of delegates from accommodation to conference venue and vice versa are included with free breakfast and lunch.
  • The Accommodation will be provided in Fully furnished, Fully Air Conditioned 2 BHK luxury Apartments with pool area. (Please refer to the pictures)
  • Occupancy is 6/7 people per 2 BHK flat.
  • Accommodation for staying an extra day will be concerned on the spot based on the availability of rooms on that date.
  • Participants are advised to refrain from booking other hotels as the accommodation provided by us is the most reasonably priced accommodation you can get in peak tourist season Goa and is situated only 2km from the conference venue.
  • Please feel free to mail at info@bitsmungoa.in in case of any queries.

Click here for the link to the payment portal. The payment process is as follows-
  1. Select "State of Corporate/Institution" as Goa.

  2. Select "Type of Corporate/Institution" as Educational Institutions.

  3. Click on Go.

  4. Select "Educational Institutions Name" as Birla Institute Of Technology and Science.

  5. Press Submit.

  6. Select "Payment Category" as BITSMUN Accommodation.

  7. Fill up the form and enter amount as INR 2200.

  8. Please note: Enter the name of your College 'space' city instead of ','(comma) in the 'Name of the College, City' tab.

  9. Press Submit and pay using the options available.

  10. If due to any reason your transaction fails, please try again using an SBI Card or Netbanking. If problem still persists, contact us ASAP at info@bitsmungoa.in
  11. Please fill the following form after a succesful payment for us to know some important details regarding your accommodation.

    Accommodation Details Form




Once the payment is successfully submitted, make a note of the transaction id and save the screenshot/printout of the transaction ticket.

Please note that once paid, the fee will not be refunded under any circumstances. Delegates should also ensure that the payment should be 2200 INR.
