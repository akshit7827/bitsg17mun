Instructions to pay

Applicants have to pay the registration fees of INR 1700. This is to confirm your application for the specified country/state. Accommodation fees will be communicated later. (Applicants are advised to use netbanking or an SBI card as their mode of payment if their transaction fails using any other bank's card.)

Click here for the link to the payment portal. The payment process is as follows-


  1. Select "State of Corporate/Institution" as Goa

  2. Select "Type of Corporate/Institution" as Educational Institutions.

  3. Click on Go.

  4. Select "Educational Institutions Name" as Birla Institute Of Technology and Science.

  5. Press Submit.

  6. Select "Payment Category" as BITSMUN Registration.

  7. Fill up the form and enter amount as 1700.

  8. Please note: Enter the name of your College 'space' city instead of ',' (comma) in the 'College' tab.

  9. Press Submit and pay using the options available.


Once the payment is successfully submitted, make a note of the transaction id and save the screenshot/printout of the transaction ticket.

Please note that once paid, the fee will not be refunded under any circumstances. Delegates should also ensure that the payment should be 1700 INR.
