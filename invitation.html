From the Secretary General - 2017

Honourable Delegates and Advisors,

It is my distinct pleasure to invite you to the 9th edition of BITS Model United Nations Conference, 3rd to 5th February, 2017 hosted by the BITSMUN Society of the BITS Pilani KK Birla Goa Campus.

BITSMUN at its creation in 2009, hosted leading delegates from across the country. Over the years the event evolved to be an event of international repute, regularly hosting delegates from across the world. Touted as India's fastest growing international MUN, BITSMUN hosts the crème de la crème of the International MUN circuit, hosting delegates from India, Italy, Germany, Belgium, Indonesia, Nepal, Kyrgyzstan, Bangladesh as well as from African Nations such as Kenya and South Africa, bringing together up to 350+ dynamic young minds annually, to deliberate on some of the world’s most pressing issues.

I have been involved in the MUN Circuit since 2010 having attended dozens of MUNs in school before I joined BITS Goa. My BITSMUN experience began in my freshman year when I was in the Executive Board of the United Nations Human Rights Council and I can say without any reservations that my time and experiences as a part of BITSMUN over the last 2 years have been the most enjoyable and rewarding of my college career and the experience of serving as the Secretary-General has been no different. I have had the privilege of working alongside hundreds of dedicated BITSians and external advisors with a nearly infinite range of backgrounds and a common passion for MUN.

After the unprecedented success of BITSMUN’16, my Secretariat and I have been working tirelessly to ensure that BITSMUN’17 will provide delegates with the most exciting, unique, and rewarding experience to be had in the Model UN circuit. BITSMUN’17 will take place in the beautiful BITS Goa campus located in South Goa renowned for its beautiful untouched beaches, nightlife and Portuguese architecture.

At BITSMUN, we negate the doctrines that segregate participants of the basis of quantity of MUNs they have participated. We strongly believe in our guiding principle which motivates us to provide equal opportunities to all those who seek it. We encourage you to indulge in your first Model United Nations Experience this BITSMUN!

Our delegates are the most integral part of our story and I'd like to once again thank you for choosing to be a part of our next chapter of BITSMUN Goa. I am certain that you will walk away from this conference with memories that you will cherish for a long time to come. If you have any questions, concerns, or comments between now and the conference, I encourage you to reach out to us using the contact tab on the website. On behalf of the entire Secretariat, I cannot wait to host you in Goa this February.


Ayush Sharma
 Secretary General
BITSMUN’17
